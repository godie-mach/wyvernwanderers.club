## Overview
##### "Mountain Terror"
This dragon lived on peak of [[Sword Mountains]]. The dragon killed [[Zog Bloodfang]] and left the [[Bloodfang Clan]] without a proper chief. Xerov was later killed by [[Rockgar Bloodfang]] and [[The Adventurers]] so [[Rockgar Bloodfang|Rockgar]] could regain chief status among the [[Bloodfang Clan]].
